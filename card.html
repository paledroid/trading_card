Leopard

Leopards are very solitary and spend most of their time alone.

  • Scientific name: Panthera pardus
  • Average length: 4.25-6.25ft, excl. tail
  • Average lifespan: 12-15 years in the wild
  • Habitat: Africa, Asia, India and China

Leopards are comfortable in trees as well as being strong swimmmers. They are graceful and powerful cats with strong hunting skills (National Geographic)
